heyo! welcome 2 my little corner of the internet!

i’m a senior at uc berkeley studying human computer interaction and critical theory. my interests span from cscw, the future of work, virtual communities, tangible UI, civic media, to cosmetic computing. anyway, what really gets me going is that i can help people with the work that i do!

currently, i work with professor Niloufar Salehi at the iSchool and professor Björn Hartmann at BiD. this summer, i’ll be doing research with Mark Whiting and professor Michael Bernstein at Stanford HCI.

feel free to take a lil’ peep of my work!
